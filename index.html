About

At Roommate To Survive, we understand the challenges of finding the perfect roommate and a suitable place to live. That's why we created a platform that brings together individuals seeking roommates and those offering shared accommodation. Our story began when Clive Sasaka from Kenya and Helen Zeleke from Ethiopia joined forces to address the pressing housing problem in our respective countries, particularly in urban centers. Driven by our personal experiences and a shared vision, we set out to develop a user-friendly and efficient solution that connects people looking for roommates with available living spaces. RoomConnect revolutionizes the roommate-finding process by leveraging advanced algorithms to match compatible individuals based on their preferences, lifestyle, and values. With RoomConnect, you can say goodbye to endless searching and awkward interviews. Our platform streamlines the process, making it easy to find like-minded individuals and secure a comfortable living arrangement. Whether you're a student, young professional, or anyone seeking a compatible roommate, we've got you covered. Join us today and embark on a stress-free journey towards finding your ideal roommate and a place to call home. Together, we can transform the way people connect, share, and thrive in vibrant urban communities.
